SyncTabs is a cross-browser tab synchronization tool for Chromium browsers. See every open tab across Chrome, Edge, Brave, Vivaldi, and Opera from a single popup — and switch to any tab with one click.

No account. No cloud. Your data stays on your machine.

## Key features

- **Real-time tab sync** across multiple browsers and windows
- **Tab persistence** — closed browser sessions stay visible
- **Send tabs** between browsers with a single click
- **End-to-end encryption** (AES-GCM 256-bit) for remote sync
- **Mobile portal** — view and send tabs from your phone
- **Dark, light, and system themes**
- **No accounts, no cloud, no data leaves your machine**
